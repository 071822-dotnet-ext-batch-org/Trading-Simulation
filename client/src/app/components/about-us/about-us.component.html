Yoink! Story

Who We Are

A group of software developers came together to create an application where everyone could learn how to make investments without risking their own money.

Yoink! Method

What We Do

We exist to help our users achieve their long-term financial goals. We believe our users should have open access to the information they need to invest with confidence. We aim to offer our users a platform where they can learn about making investments through simulation.

Yoink! Mission

Why We Do It

Investing can cause valid and genuine fears for new investors. Even experienced investors can become scared at times. People make bad decisions, get carried away by emotions, and lose money because of situations outside of their control. Yoink! gives you the opportunity to experience investing without any of the these risks.
